Infinite Objects

A voice-driven, agentic AI experience where visitors collaborate with specialized AI agents, to concept, design and build a new product in 10 minutes.

A voice-driven multi-modal agents AI workflow

Infinite Objects reframes the process of industrial design as a conversation. Instead of starting with constraints, participants begin with instinct — an idea, a curiosity, a frustration, or a desire. As the dialogue unfolds, a coordinated system of AI agents works in parallel to:

  • Identify whitespace and unmet needs
  • Clarify the target customer and use case
  • Explore materials, form factors, and manufacturing considerations
  • Shape value, pricing, and differentiation
  • Translate intent into a resolved product concept

What once required teams, tools, and long timelines now begins with a single voice and a point of view.

Invention at the speed of human imagination

How It Works

A guided, voice-first workflow designed for uninhibited creativity and efficiency.

Imagine

Start with a rough idea. Participants describe what they want to make, why it matters, or the problem it solves.

Collaborate

Behind the scenes, specialized AI agents reason across marketing, engineering, legal and design to decide brand intent, market context, manufacturing and materials — asking smart follow-ups and refining the concept in real time.

Define

The system converges on a clear product direction: name, positioning, audience, and value proposition.

Visualize

The idea is brought to life through an evolving polished visual representation that reflects the intended form, style, and use.

Launch the idea

Participants leave with a fully articulated 3D render of their product concept, designed to spark discussion, validation, and next steps.

Why It Matters

Collapsing the Distance Between Thought and Execution

Every meaningful new product begins as a moment of curiosity. Historically, the hardest part was not the idea — but everything required to make it real.

With agentic AI, that distance collapses.

For the first time, the ability to make begins to match the scale and speed of human imagination — opening design, invention, and innovation to more people, faster than ever before.

Making Ideas Visible, Fast

A critical shift enabled by Infinite Objects is the ability to see ideas as they form. Instead of stopping at language or abstract descriptions, concepts are translated into clear, legible visual representations that express form, proportion, material intent, and personality. These visuals are not treated as final designs, but as thinking tools — artifacts that help teams align, react, and refine.

By generating product representations in real time:

  • Ideas become easier to evaluate and discuss
  • Intent is shared instantly across disciplines
  • Feedback moves from opinion to something concrete
  • Momentum is preserved instead of lost in translation

Visual output acts as a bridge between imagination and decision-making — helping brands move forward faster, with clarity and confidence.

The Technology Behind the Experience

The Infinite Objects experience integrates Adobe and other AI technologies to create a seamless, real-time workflow:

  • Adobe Workflow Builder — Node-based interface for building generative workflows that technical creatives can self-service without a dev resource
  • Adobe Firefly Services — Powers the Workflow Builder back-end
  • Adobe Firefly Partner Models — Power the image generations
  • Adobe App Builder — Powers custom nodes in Workflow Builder
  • Adobe Substance 3D API — Renders 3D turntable animations
  • Adobe Experience Manager Edge Delivery Services — Product gallery
  • Adobe Journey Optimizer — Orchestrates and personalizes customer journeys across every touchpoint
  • Anthropic Claude — Multi-agent architecture, enabling an orchestration agent to coordinate specialized sub-agents across Design, Engineering, Marketing, and Legal
  • ElevenLabs — Enables AI voice customization and audio-driven interactions powered by Claude
  • Meshy — Creates and renders 3D models for product visualization and spatial asset development
  • Vercel / Next.js — Powers the real-time web experience connecting the conversational interface, generative pipeline, and product gallery
